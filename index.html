“What’s Keeping You Motivated”

What is streaming?

Streaming is a method of watching a recorded/live video or audio content that is uploaded into the internet for audiences to view, and get entertainment. Streaming content varies from music to gaming videos, but for one to upload content to the internet, one must use a streaming platform which could be Youtube, Twitch, TikTok, Spotify, etc.

The main goal of our website

The main goal of our website is to inform audiences why certain people watch certain streaming categories in hopes for them to understand why they also enjoy specific content/s uploaded onto the internet.

Our streaming category

The streaming category that the team decided to choose was the streaming of video essays. The reason we chose this streaming category is that there is a wide variety of younger audiences and also it is an informative way to make content that resonates with us, students.

What are “Video Essays”?

Video Essays refers to analyzing a specific or certain topic that is related to a film or television. It can be created by writing, audio, and visual for the partakers to understand the whole context being discussed.

Procedure on how to get the data

To get the data results, the entire team decided that interviewing individuals who particularly enjoy video essays would be the most effective way to understand why they enjoy watching video essays on streaming platforms.

Data Results

By gathering data from our participants we have seen and compiled the answers given. With the question of “What do you like about video essays?” 5/5 or 100% of our respondents said that they like Video Essays and the topic. The next question was “What makes you watch these types of videos?” 5/5 or 100% of our respondents watch these types of videos to get entertained. And the last question was “What type of topics in video essays interest you?” 5/5 or 100% of our respondents said that the topic they like is about opinions on certain topics like films, movies, and etc.

What do we learn from this?

Video essays are very diverse in their topic and because of this, they can interest or connect dozens of viewers online. What we can get from the data results is that entertainment is very essential in today’s society with the pandemic limiting our movement not only is it fun to watch/listen to someone that talks about a specific wide variety of topics, but also because it can also help us academically by observing how a certain content creator break down the topic.
